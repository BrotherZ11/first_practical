## Business Context

FinTech Corp is a rapidly growing financial technology company providing digital payment solutions to 50,000+ active users. With increasing regulatory scrutiny and sophisticated cyber threats, the company's minimal security posture has become a critical business risk.

### Current Challenges

1. **Limited Security Team**: Only 3 security personnel
   - PD-WRL-001: Basic SIEM/EDR alert monitoring
   - PD-WRL-003: Reactive incident handling
   - IO-WRL-005: General IT operations with partial security duties

2. **Critical Threat Exposure**:
   - **Ransomware Attacks**: 0% defensive coverage
   - **Supply Chain Compromises**: 0% detection capability
   - **Data Leakage**: Minimal DLP controls
   - **Audit Failures**: Insufficient compliance documentation

3. **Regulatory Pressure**:
   - PCI DSS compliance gaps
   - GDPR/privacy requirements
   - SOC 2 Type II certification needed

## Optimization Strategy

### NICE Framework Alignment

The optimization uses the **NICE Cybersecurity Workforce Framework** to map:

- **Tasks (T)**: Specific cybersecurity activities
- **Knowledge (K)**: Required understanding areas
- **Skills (S)**: Technical and operational competencies

### Focus: SOC (Security Operations Center)

**Weighting Strategy:**

- Tasks: 100% (prioritize operational capabilities)
- Skills: 60% (balanced technical depth)
- Knowledge: 30% (foundational understanding)

This SOC-focused approach prioritizes immediate threat detection and response capabilities over strategic planning roles.
