#### Action Breakdown

| Action Type              | Count   | Budget | Rationale                                                   |
| ------------------------ | ------- | ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hire (FTE)**           | 4 roles | $140K  | Core capacity (SOC analysts, GRC lead, Threat analyst)      |

- Hiring builds **long-term capacity** for 24/7 operations

- Outsourcing provides **immediate expertise** for compliance gaps

## Slide 10: Closing — The Narrative

### **Why This Matters: A Story of Resilience**

**Where We Are:**  
A lean cybersecurity team doing heroic work but stretched beyond sustainable limits. We're one major incident away from significant financial and reputational damage.

**Where We're Going:**  
A right-sized, skilled, data-driven security organization aligned to industry standards (NICE Framework). Capable of detecting, responding to, and preventing the threats that matter most to our business.

**How We Get There:**  
Not with more tools or more FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt), but with **people who have the right skills doing the right tasks**. This plan is built on science: optimization algorithms, workforce frameworks, and risk quantification.

**The Board's Role:**  
Approve the investment that protects our customers' data, our regulatory standing, and our ability to operate without disruption.

✅ Expected annual loss from cyber events: $788K. Failed audits: likely within 18 months. Customer trust erosion: unquantifiable but real.
